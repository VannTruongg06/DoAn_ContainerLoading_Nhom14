# Du An Container Loading - Nhom 14

## Gioi thieu
Day la du an giai quyet bai toan xep hang vao container su dung cac thuat toan DP, Greedy va Genetic Algorithm.

## Cau truc thu muc
- `src/`: Chua ma nguon C++
- `data/`: Chua file input va output
- `docs/`: Chua bao cao va thuyet trinh

## Cach bien dich va chay
Dung g++ de bien dich:
```bash
g++ src/main.cpp src/knapsack_dp.cpp src/greedy_packing.cpp src/genetic_algorithm.cpp -o main
./main
```
